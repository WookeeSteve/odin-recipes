Hoisin Beef

A spicy, sweet and smoky meat dish complemented with a good heaping of rice

Ingredients

  • Garlic Clove
  • Basmati Rice
  • Beef Mince
  • Thai Style Spice
  • Hoisin Sauce
  • Honey
  • 100ml Water

Steps

  1. Chop garlic
  2. Boil rice
  3. Start cooking beef in pan at a medium-high heat
  4. Once beef has browned at garlic
  5. After 3 minutes lower heat to medium and add spices and sauce
  6. Add water and allow to simmer
  7. Once most of the water has evaporated, stir in tablespoon of honey
  8. Serve with rice and allow to sit
