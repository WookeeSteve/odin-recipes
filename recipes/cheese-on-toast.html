Cheese On Toast

A good snack for when you're feeling a bit peckish

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices Of Bread
  • Cheddar Cheese (sliced)
  • Spring Onion
  • Worcester Sauce

Steps

  1. Toast one side of bread under a medium heat grill
  2. Add onion on untoasted side
  3. Place cheese on top of onions
  4. Sprinkle Worcester Sauce on top of cheese
  5. Grill until cheese is well melted and browned
