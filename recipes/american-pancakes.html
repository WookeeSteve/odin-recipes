American Pancakes

Fluffy pancakes with a hint of ginger for a special treat at breakfast time

Ingredients

  • 100g Plain Flour
  • 25g Corn Flour
  • 35g Brown Sugar
  • 2/3rds Cup Of Milk
  • 1 Egg
  • 2 Tablespoons of Ginger
  • Honey

Steps

  1. Mix flours, sugar and ginger together and make a well
  2. Beat egg
  3. Add egg and milk to mixture and mix well
  4. (optionally let sit for 30 minutes)
  5. Pour into medium heat and sprinkle with brown sugar
  6. When bubbles have formed on top flip
  7. Make sure they're properly cooked well brown
  8. Spread honey on top
